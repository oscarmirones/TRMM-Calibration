This file contains relevant information about trends_data.csv

Columns:

	Date: Complete date of the corresponding record

	Year: Year of the corresponding record

	Month: Month of the corresponding record

	Day: Day of the corresponding record

	WT: Weather Type attributed to the corresponding record

	Train_or_Test: Indicates if the corresponding record has been used in train or test phase.
			Period 1979-1997 was used as test and 1998-2020 as train.
	
	SPI: Corresponding value of SPI to the record. Only computed for train period

	Tertile: Indicates the SPI tertile to which each record corresponds. Only computed for train period
